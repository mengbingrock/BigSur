Think out loud.

What it does

A workspace that thinks alongside you.

Not a chat box that forgets, and not a rigid tool that boxes you in — a place where the model's reasoning becomes something you can touch.

01

Conversational orchestration

Chat with Claude as it loads the right research skills and protocols and runs them for you — the conductor, not the instrument.

02

A living canvas

Every reply becomes an editable graph: phases, sub-steps, and decision points you can drag, rename, connect, and drill into.

03

Comment & steer

Box-select any part of the plan, attach a sticky note, and submit it back — the model responds knowing exactly what you meant.

04

Skills & artifacts, local

A searchable catalog of skills and user-authored protocols, each documenting what it does and which tools it orchestrates. Your data stays on your machine.

The canvas

Where the answer becomes editable.

Phases flow left to right. Choices gate what comes next. Comments tether to whatever you selected — and a click sends your note, plus its context, back into the conversation.

How it works

Ask, steer, ship.

Step 01

Ask

Describe your research task in plain language. Labee picks the skills and protocols that fit.

Step 02

Steer

The reply unfolds on the canvas. Edit phases, pick options, drop comments, and submit refinements.

Step 03

Ship

Artifacts and decks are saved locally. Close the app, reopen, and pick up exactly where you left off.

Native desktop

Runs on your machine.
Your data never leaves it.

Labee ships as a real desktop app for macOS and Windows — the full workspace, packaged with its own server, storing everything in your local user folder.

macOS Windows

Built with Electron. Unsigned local builds may show an OS warning on first launch.
